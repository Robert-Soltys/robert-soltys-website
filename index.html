About

Robert Soltys is a graphic designer practicing out of Spokane, Washington but has spent a majority of his practice in Chicago, Illinois. Robert is schooled in Bauhaus design esthetic philosophy emphasizing function follows form. His expertise is in design theory, type setting, layout hierarchy, typography, UI/UX, and web development. Robert is highly entrepreneurial and always looking to take on the next big idea or project.
